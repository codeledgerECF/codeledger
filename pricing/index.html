Truth Audit Certificates

A signed record of what your AI coding assistant actually did.

AI coding tools do not produce audit trails. Truth Audit does. Each certificate captures which files the agent read, what changed, what it skipped, and what evidence was checked — then signs it so you can hand it to procurement, security, or an acquirer without sending source code.
