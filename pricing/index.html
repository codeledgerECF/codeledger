What is CodeLedger?

CodeLedger is the accountability layer for AI coding tools. It tracks which files your AI agent read, what it changed, and whether there is evidence it got things right. The result: your team ships AI-assisted code with confidence instead of guessing whether it worked.

For developers

The CLI picks the right files for any task — no manual hunting, no wasted context.

For engineering leads

See which AI agents are adding risk, which are saving time, and where the codebase is drifting.

For CTOs and security teams

Get a signed certificate proving what the AI coding system did — and what evidence exists for it.

Prompt Coach · Signal Architecture

The advice gets better as your team uses it more

Before your AI agent starts on a task, the Prompt Coach checks whether the request is clear enough to succeed — catching missing context, scope contradictions, and risky patterns upfront. On Free it uses a curated industry baseline. On Team it learns from your repo's own history. The quality of that coaching improves at every tier.
